Vision

Ams Media is Hanoi - Amsterdam High School For the Gifted’s only media club, directly under the school’s newspaper. Ams Media captures and treasures the memories and stories of the students during their time at Hanoi - Amsterdam High School. The club also acts as a means to connect Amsers to other students all over the nation. Every event and activity carried out by the school and the pupils will be documented through pictures and reports on Ams Media’s page.

My role

- Lead 30 members
- Provides overall leadership and direction to the club organization
- Establishes short- and long- range objectives and goals in conjunction with the board of directors
- Coordinates club activities through the board of directors
- Presides at meetings of the club
- Structures the organization to ensure continuity by providing opportunities for new leadership to develop
- Exercises overall financial responsibility for the club
- Approves all club press releases, newsletters, communications, and other publications
