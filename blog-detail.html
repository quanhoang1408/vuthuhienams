About Dash for Impact

As a project of AIESEC in Vietnam established in 2012, Dash For Impact responsibility of leading high school students to create playgrounds that both develop soft skills and contribute to the creation of new challenges. The project has positive implications for the community.

"We eliminated 400 contestants to be the finalist."

On that journey of making an impact, Dash for Impact has inspired more than 2000+ young people across the country and created more than 18 projects into operation.

Continuing the journey of inspiring students to take action for the community and towards the 17 sustainable development goals of the United Nations, Dash for Impact 2021 organized the "COMMUNICATION PROJECT PLANNING CONTEST" combined with ONLINE WORKSHOPs in order to equip students with knowledge in the field of communication, development of leadership, teamwork and critical thinking, etc., as well as decision-making. towards the development of students on the way to conquer social projects
